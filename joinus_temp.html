    Teaching

    The Bren School offers two Masters degrees in Environmental Science & Management (MESM) and Environmental Data Science (MEDS). Our group is heavily involved in the MEDS program and also advises in the MESM program. The MEDS program is growing rapidly and we're happy to answer any questions.

    Research

    Our group is growing and working to build a supportive and collaborative lab community. There are opportunities to join existing projects and develop new ideas together. If you're interested in joining, please fill out this interest survey instead of emailing.

    PhD Students

    PhD students can join the group through the Bren School's PhD program and should reach out via the interest form to establish connections prior to applying.

    Here are some great resources on navigating the PhD application process and tips on how to craft your message:

    Postdoctoral researchers

    Postdoctoral researchers are especially encouraged to reach out with ideas that could spark fellowship applications. Our group participates in the Bren Global Change Postdoctoral Fellowship which offers opportunities for community building and career development. If our research interests align, please reach out via the interest form.
